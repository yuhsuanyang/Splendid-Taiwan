Eastern Taiwan is with sceneries of fascinating mountains and waters. Located at the junction of the Philippine Sea Plate and the Eurasian Plate, the plate tectonics results in moutain peaks and cliffs. Agriculture is the main industry here, and rural landscapes come into view in the plain area. In this region, you can get rid of the hustle and bustle of the city, and enjoy the magnificence of the natural sceneries!
