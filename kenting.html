Kenting

Pingtung County

Bai-Sha Bay

Kenting National Park is surrounded by the Pacific Ocean, the Taiwan Strait, and the Luzon Strait,covering Hengchun,
Checheng, and Manzhou Townships. It is well known for its tropical climate and sunshine, scenic mountain, beach and
coral reefs. Beaches sush as Nan Wan, Banana Bay and Bai-Sha Bay is famous for the water sports. The park hosts rich
terrestrial biodiversity of mammals, birds, freshwater fish and various insects. During spring and winter, the strong
katabatic wind blows through this area, which boosts the growth of onions. Moreover, you can enjoy the fresh seafoods
and even buy some at the market near the fishery harbors.

  • Official Website: http://www.ktnp.gov.tw/en/Default.aspx
  • Best seasons: Summer
  • Transportation:
    Frequent buses link this park with Kaohsiung International Airport,
    Taiwan Railway Administration's Kaohsiung Station and Taiwan High Speed Rail's Zuoying Station.
    One-way ride takes around 2 hours and 30 minutes.
