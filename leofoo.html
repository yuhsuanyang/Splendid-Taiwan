Leofoo Village Theme Park

Hsinchu County

Leofoo Village Theme Park

TThe Leofoo Village Theme Park is an amusement park and a safari located in Guanxi Township. It was originally
established in 1979 as Leofoo Wildlife Park. It 1989, the wildlife park was reestablished to Leofoo Village Theme Park.
The park is devided in four sections, including Arabian Kingdom, African Safari, South Pacific and Wild West.
There are different amusement facilities in each section. Among them, the African Safari section is a wild
safari-themed area, featuring many animal-interactive attractions.

  • Official Website: http://www1.leofoo.com.tw/village/en/
  • Best seasons: all year around
  • Transportation:
    Take Taiwan Railway trains to Chungli Station, and switch to Hsinchu Bus #5653 to reach Leofoo Village Theme Park.
