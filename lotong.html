Lotung

Ilan County

Lotong Sports Park

Lotung Township is an urban township in the central part of Yilan County. Although it is the smallest township
in the county, it is the best-known area. Lotong Sports Park is the most famous tourist spot in Lotong.
The park covers 47 hectares of land and it includes a range of facilities for sport and a swimming pool.
The park is divided into four sections that include; forest area, sport area, waterscapes, and other landscapes such as
lakes and the taxodium which turns yellow during the winter. Lotung Nightmarket is one of the largest and crowed
nightmarket in Taiwan. The night market offers local snacks such as such as ox-tongue-shaped pastry and
iced tapioca with red bean stuffed.

  • Best seasons: all year around
  • Transportation: Luodong is served by the Yilan Line of Taiwan Railway Administration at the Luodong Station.
